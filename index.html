Hi there, I'm Thiemo.

I'm a PhD student at the Institute of Information Systems at University of St.Gallen (CH) advised by Jan Marco Leimeister.

I strive to conduct interdisciplinary research in the fields of Information Systems, Computer Linguistics, and Human-Computer-Interaction to increase learning success and education across all domains. To do so, I use techniques from Artificial Intelligence such as Natural Language Processing, Transfer and Deep Learning to build AI-powered education tools such as Intelligent-Tutoring-Systems or Conversational Agents. You can find information on my work on AI for Education

As part of a reserach team, we have developed an adaptive Learning Support System for Argumentation Skills which has beneficial use for developing better writing and reasoning for students than traditional learning methods. (Link to CHI 2020 paper) Most recently, I have desgined ArgueTutor, an intelligent conversational agent, that provides students with adpative and individual argumentation tutoring while writing persuasive essays indepdent of an instructor, time or location. (Link to CHI 2021 paper)

I believe in sharing and collaboration for building better systems. If you are interested in my work and ideas please contact me via LinkedIn

Corpora and Data Sets

In order to build HCI innnovations, I collectede, annotated and scientifically evaluated different corpora and data sets to train novel machine learning models. Here is an overview of my work:

  • German User-Generated Repair Instruction Corpus: 3,157 user-generated posts, with 682 tagged “Instruction” and 2,475 tagged “NoInstruction”, representing different characteristics of the platform motor-talk. Access
  • Argumentation Annotated Student Peer Reviews Corpus: 1000 persuasive student peer reviews about business model feedbacks annotated for their argumentative components and argumentative relations Access
  • Argumentation Annotated Student Businss Model Pitch Corpus: 500 persuasive business model pitches annotated for argumentative components, argumentative quality score and argumentative relations
  • Cognitive and Emotional Empathy Annotated Student Peer Reviews Corpus: 500 persuasive student peer revies about business model feedbacks annotated for their cognitve and emotional empathy level according to key constructs
