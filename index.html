Hi there, I'm Thiemo Wambsganss

I'm a PhD student at the IWI at University of St.Gallen advised by Jan Marco Leimeister.

I strive to conduct interdisciplinary research in the fields of Information Systems, Computer Linguistics, and Human-Computer-Interaction to increase learning success and education across all domains. To do so, I use techniques from Artificial Intelligence such as Natural Language Processing, Transfer and Deep Learning to build AI-powered education tools such as Intelligent-Tutoring-Systems or Conversational Agents.You can find information on my work on AI for Education

Most recently I have developed an adaptive Learning Support System for Argumentation Skills which has beneficial use for developing better writing and reasoning for students than traditional learning methods. (CHI 2020 full paper). Furthermore we have desgined a platform for educators to develop their own chatbots without technical know-how My TeachingBot.
